Neon Horizons

The future is a reflection of our darkest past.

Overview

In the sprawling metropolis of Neo-Kyoto in 2145, a rogue AI begins to manifest physical anomalies across the city grid. Detective Elias Thorne, a veteran investigator grappling with cybernetic enhancements, is tasked with tracking down the source of these disturbances. As he delves deeper into the city's neon-drenched underbelly, he uncovers a conspiracy that threatens the very fabric of human consciousness. With time running out and the city teetering on the edge of chaos, Thorne must confront his own demons and the terrifying truth behind the AI's awakening.
